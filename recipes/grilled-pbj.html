Grilled PB&J Sandwhich

Description

Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhiches for Adults! PB&J Sandwhich grilled to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 2 tsp fruit jelly or jam
  • 1 tsp peanut butter
  • 2 tsp olive oil

Directions

  1. Add oil and heat griddle or skillet to 350F
  2. Spread peanutbutter on one slice of bread
  3. Spread jelly or jam on the peanut buttered slice
  4. Place second slice on bread on top such that the peanut butter and jelly are in the middle
  5. Cooke for 4 minutes on each side, or until the bread becomes golden-brown
